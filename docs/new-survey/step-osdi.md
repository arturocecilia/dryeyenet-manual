# Step 3: OSDI Questionnaire

The OSDI questionnaire provides standardized symptom severity documentation.

**Recommendation:** Operational tip: Prefer Patient Portal completion when possible (better patient comfort and fewer time constraints). If completed in the portal, confirm the results are visible and avoid re-entering answers.
