# 1.1 Base Survey

The **New Base Survey Workflow** is used to document a baseline exam for a patient. It is designed to be practical in busy clinics: you can add/edit patient data inside the flow, and you can cancel at any time.

:::tip
If you are interrupted frequently, complete one step at a time and click **Next** to ensure your progress is recorded.
:::
