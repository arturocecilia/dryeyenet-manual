# Step 2: Attach Local Files to the Patient Documents

After selecting the patient, you can proceed to choose the device exam documents stored on your computer that you want to attach to the patient.

![Attach Device Exam Documents](/img/import-device-exam/impDevExa-attachFile_en.png)

You can cancel the import at any time:

![Cancel Document Import](/img/import-device-exam/impDevExa-cancelImport_en.png)

Once the required documents have been imported, click `SAVE` to attach them to the patient record.

![Save Documents to Patient](/img/import-device-exam/impDevExa-bottomMenu_en.png)
