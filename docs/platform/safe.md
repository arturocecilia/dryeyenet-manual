# DryEyeNet SAFE

**DryEyeNet SAFE** is the name given to the Patient Portal within the DryEyeNet system. All patients are granted access to the same set of functionalities, which include:

- **Access to their clinical documents**
- **Ability to complete and submit Base Survey information**
- **Access to direct contact information for their care provider**
