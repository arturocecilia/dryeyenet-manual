# Creating or Ordering a Treatment Plan Depending on the Role of the Eye Care Professional

> **Remember:** **DryEyeNet**, as a comanagement system, supports different workflows and allows each professional to perform all or part of the care flow.

Regarding the **Treatment Plan**, an **order** is required before it can be carried out. Depending on your role, you may order a treatment plan for the patient, perform a treatment plan, or do both.

**Information contained**

- **Treatment cycle number**
- **General tasks**, such as patient education or blink training
- **OTC medication**
- **Auxiliary means**
- **Professional eyelid margin treatments**
- **Prescription drugs**
- **Other**, such as amniotic membrane or scleral contact lenses
