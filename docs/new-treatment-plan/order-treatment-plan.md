# Create Treatment Plan Order

- **1. Open the Patient Dashboard and select the patient**

To create a treatment plan order:

1. Go to the Patient Dashboard.
2. Select the patient for whom you want to request the treatment plan.

![Substep Select the patient in the Patient Dashboard](/img/new-treatment-plan/newTrePla_requestOrder_en.png)

- **2. Click the _Request Treatment Plan_ button**

To make the **Request Treatment Plan** option available in the bottom menu, the patient must have at least a **Base Survey** available.  
The **Base Exam** and **Diagnostic Tests** may also be present, but they are not mandatory.

![Substep Click the button to request the treatment plan](/img/new-treatment-plan/newTrePla_patBottomMenu_en.png)

- **3. Fill out the additional required information for the treatment plan order**

Once you click the button, a screen will appear showing the documents that will be available to the professional assigning the treatment plan.  
You will also have the option to add free text.

![Substep Review the Treatment Plan request and add information if needed](/img/new-treatment-plan/newTrePla_proceedWithRequest_en.png)
