# 1.1 Base Survey

The **New Base Survey** workflow is used to document a patientâ€™s baseline assessment. It is designed to be practical in busy clinics: you can add or edit patient data within the workflow, and you can cancel at any time.

You can access **New Base Survey** from the **Main Menu**:

![New Base Survey in the Main Menu](/img/new-base-survey/newBasSur_maiMen_en.png)

The first step is to [**select an existing patient**](/docs/new-base-survey/step-select-patient) or create a new one (`ADD PATIENT`) in order to complete the base survey.

The areas included in the Base Survey are:

- **Anamnesis**

  - General risk factors
  - Disorder risk factors
  - Ocular history
  - Eye drops / ointments
  - Eye medication
  - Regular systemic medication
  - General medication
  - Nutrient / gene-related factors
  - Demographic details

- **OSDI**

- **Visual Acuity & Remarks**

- **Import Document**

> **Tip:** If you are interrupted frequently, complete one step at a time and click **Next** to ensure your progress is saved.
