# Select a Patient

After clicking `New Treatment` in the **Main Menu**, a list of all patients will be displayed.

![List of patients](/img/new-treatment/newTre_selectPat_en.png)

# Select a Treatment Plan

If the patient has one or more treatment plans assigned, you will be able to select the treatment plan you want to document.

![List of treatment plans assigned to the patient](/img/new-treatment/newTre_selectTrePlan_en.png)
