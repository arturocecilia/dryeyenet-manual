# Patients Can Fill Out Their Base Survey in the Patient Portal

Click `NEW BASE SURVEY` in the **Main Menu** and then select the patient. The next **action menu** will appear:

![Action menu with patient selected in New Base Survey workflow](/img/patient-portal/patPor_basSur_sendTo.png)

If you click `Send to Patient Portal`, the **Base Survey** will automatically appear once the patient logs in.

> **Tip:** You can simultaneously send the Patient Portal invitation to the patient, and the Base Survey will automatically open upon login.

![Action menu with patient selected in New Base Survey workflow](/img/patient-portal/patPor_basSur_invite.png)
