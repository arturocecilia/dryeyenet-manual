# Give Access to Patient Portal

After creating the patient, you can invite them by clicking `INVITE TO THE PATIENT PORTAL` in the [**Patient Dashboard**](/docs/patient-dashboard/intro).

![Bottom Menu in the Patient Dashboard](/img/patient-portal/patPor_giveAccess_en.png)

By clicking this option, an email will be sent to the address provided when [creating the patient](/docs/patient-management/create-patient).  
The email contains a link directing the patient to a form where they can complete their access details.
