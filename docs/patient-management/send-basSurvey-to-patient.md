# Patients Can Fill Out Their Base Survey in the Patient Portal

Click `NEW BASE SURVEY` in the **Main Menu** and select the patient.  
The next **Action Menu** will appear:

![Action menu with patient selected in New Base Survey workflow](/img/patient-portal/patPor_basSur_sendTo.png)

When you click **`Send to Patient Portal`**, the **Base Survey** will automatically be available to the patient the next time they log in.

:::tip
You can send the Patient Portal invitation at the same time.  
If you do so, the Base Survey will automatically open when the patient logs in.
:::

![Action menu with patient selected in New Base Survey workflow](/img/patient-portal/patPor_basSur_invite.png)
